Peach Cobbler

Description

A healthy and tasty recipe for peach cobbler.

Ingredients

  • For the filling
  • 3.5 cups peaches--slightly overripe
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/6 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 2 tsp cornstarch
  • 1 tsp almond extract
  • For the topping
  • 3/4 cup white whole wheat or gluten free flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 1/4 cup plain nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup pure maple syrup
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Prep the peaches: Optional to peel them or not. Very thinly slice. Toss with the filling ingredients, until cinnamon evenly coats them and Cornstarch has dissolved.
  2. Make the topping: Do in separate bowl. Cut butter until it resembles fine crumbs. Pour in the remaining ingredients and stir until batter comes together.
  3. Assemble--and quickly!: Baking soda will change texture so work fast. Put filling in pan,press down with spatula to make even surface. Dollop batter on top, spread it across fruit, use delicate touch.
  4. Bake: It's finished when the topping looks light gold and is firm to the touch. You should see the peach juice bubbling away around the edges of the pan too.
  5. Cool and chill: Preferably at room temp for 3+ hours, but can speed up by putting in the fridge. You want the filling to be solid enough to not totally fall apart when you cut into it.
