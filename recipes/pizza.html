Pizza

Description

Healthy pizza topped with veggies, mozzarella, chicken, arugula, and a balsamic glaze. Only takes 20 minutes to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb whole wheat pizza dough, or flat bread or cauliflower crust
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup pizza sauce
  • 1/2 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/2 cup grape tomatoes, sliced
  • 1/2 cup red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1-2 cups shredded chicken--optional
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup arugula
  • balsamic glaze for drizzling

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 F.
  2. Place dough on pan/baking sheet and lightly brush olive oil on top.
  3. Sprinkle garlic cloves evenly.
  4. Lightly spread pizza sauce on top with the back of a spoon
  5. Top with sliced mushrooms, tomatoes, peppers, chicken and mozzarella. Bake until crust is golden brown and the center is cooked; about 12-15 minutes.
  6. Remove from oven, top with arugula and balsamic glaze if desired.
  7. Slice and serve.
